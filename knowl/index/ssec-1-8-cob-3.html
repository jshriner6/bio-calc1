Cob-webbing Algorithm.

Begin with the graph of an updating function \(b_{t+1} = f(b_t)\text{,}\) the diagonal line \(b_{t+1} = b_t\text{,}\) and an initial condition \(b_0\text{.}\)
  1. Identify \(b_0\) on the horizontal axis (\(b_t\)) of the graph of the updating function.
  2. Travel vertically until you intersect the updating function, and draw a closed circle.
  3. From the closed circle, travel horizontally until you intersect the diagonal line \(b_{t+1} = b_t\text{.}\)
  4. From this point on the diagonal line, travel vertically until you intersect the updating function, and draw a closed circle.
  5. Repeat step 3 through step 4 as many times as desired.
