Warm-Up 3.5.1.

Let \(f(x) = 2 + \frac{3}{1+(x+1)^2}\text{.}\)
  1. Determine all of the critical numbers of \(f\text{.}\)
  2. Construct a first derivative sign chart for \(f\) and thus determine all intervals on which \(f\) is increasing or decreasing. Classify all critical numbers from part (a) as relative maxima, relative minima, or neither.
  3. Do you think \(f\) has a global maximum? If so, why, and what is its value and where is the maximum attained? If not, explain why.
